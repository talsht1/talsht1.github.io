About

I'm a senior test automation engineer with a strong focus on engineering quality, release reliability, and testability. Over six years I've built and owned automation frameworks across the full stack — UI, API, backend, and integration — primarily in Python, TypeScript, and Java, using tools such as Playwright, Selenium, and Pytest. I integrate automated validation directly into Jenkins and GitHub Actions pipelines, and work with Docker, Kubernetes, and Terraform to deploy and manage test infrastructure at scale. I enjoy close collaboration with developers and cross-functional teams, and I'm actively growing into DevOps and platform engineering domains where robust test infrastructure intersects with production systems.
