About

I'm a senior automation and DevOps engineer with a strong focus on engineering quality, release reliability, and platform tooling. Over six years I've built and owned automation frameworks across the full stack — UI, API, backend, and integration — primarily in Python, TypeScript, and Java, using tools such as Playwright, Selenium, and Pytest. I design and maintain CI/CD pipelines in Jenkins and GitHub Actions, integrating quality gates for code coverage, vulnerability scanning, and license compliance via SonarQube, Trivy, Jit, and Black Duck. I work with Docker, Kubernetes, Helm, Terraform, and AWS CDK to build and operate infrastructure at scale, and manage artifacts and images through Google Artifact Registry and JFrog Artifactory. I enjoy close collaboration with developers and cross-functional teams and thrive at the intersection of automation, DevOps, and platform engineering.
